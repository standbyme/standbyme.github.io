Hongyu Cai

Hongyu Cai is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University. He works at the PurSec Lab under the supervision of Prof. Z. Berkay Celik. His research focuses on the security of foundation models and their applications.

Prior to joining Purdue University, he earned a bachelor's degree from Northeastern University in Shenyang, China, in 2020.
